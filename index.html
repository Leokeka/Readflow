What you will find here

  • RSVP: shows one word at a time.
  • Focus: shows the full text with a reading highlight.
  • Chunking: shows groups of words at a time.
  • Speed Test: measures time, total words, and WPM.

How to use it

Choose a tab, paste your text, adjust the speed if needed, and click start. Each mode was designed to be clear, direct, and easy to use on your computer or phone.
